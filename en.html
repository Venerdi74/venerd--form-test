Our Apartment

The Venerdì House apartment is a spacious 80 sqm flat, located on the second floor (no elevator). Two bedrooms, a living room with a fully equipped kitchen, bathroom, and balconies. Equipped with air conditioning, free WiFi, and all the amenities for a pleasant stay in Alghero.
